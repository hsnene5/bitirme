The UACT provides a service to users to control their drone in various modes.


At Autonomous Cargo Transporter, we believe there is a better way to do transportation. Our aim is to find a solution against traffic, environmental pollution, manpower usage and such different problems by designing a drone that can carry autonomous and cargo. We focus on to control this aircraft also via a web application. This system is a more valuable way where customers are earned rather than bought. Our mission is to help people use and to become widespread this new transportation system. It’s one of the least understood and least transparent aspects of transportation and we see that as an opportunity. We are excited to simplify using a drone for everyone daily routine through our software, education and originality.
